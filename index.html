Privacy Policy

Intoxicated Child Studio　·　Effective date: 2026-06-10

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications (collectively, the “Apps”) published on the Apple App Store by Intoxicated Child Studio (“we”, “us”). Each App’s “App Privacy” label on its App Store product page summarizes that App’s data practices and applies together with this policy; where they differ, the App’s label governs.

1. Our Principles

We build our Apps on a principle of data minimization. Except for the Apps listed in the App-Specific Disclosures section below (and as disclosed in each App’s App Store “App Privacy” label), our Apps:

  • do not require you to create an account, and do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number;
  • do not track you across other apps or websites, and do not use any data for advertising;
  • do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. How We Handle Data

If an App needs access to a device feature (such as the camera, microphone, photos, location, or contacts), the related data is used only on your device to provide that feature. Unless the App explicitly states otherwise, it is not uploaded to us or any third party.

App-Specific Disclosures

The general policy above describes Apps that do not collect data. Any App listed below has additional data practices; for that App, the disclosures here apply in addition to (and, where more specific, instead of) the general statements above. Each entry is consistent with that App’s App Store “App Privacy” label.

As of the effective date above, no App collects data beyond the general policy.

3. Third-Party Services

If an individual App uses third-party services (for example, system services provided by Apple, or crash-reporting and analytics tools), this is disclosed within that App or in its App Store “App Privacy” label, and the third party’s own privacy policy also applies.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the age defined by the laws of your jurisdiction).

5. Data Retention & Security

Any data generated on your device is managed by your device and its operating system. You can delete the App from your device at any time to remove its locally stored data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on the laws of your jurisdiction (such as the GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Because we generally do not collect personally identifiable information, we usually do not hold personal data to retrieve; if you have any questions, please contact us below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the content on this page and the “Effective date” above. Material changes will also be noted in the App or on its store page.

隱私權政策

Intoxicated Child Studio　·　生效日期：2026-06-10

本隱私權政策適用於 Intoxicated Child Studio(以下稱「本工作室」)在 Apple App Store 發佈的所有應用程式(以下統稱「本 App」)。每個 App 在 App Store 商店頁的「App 隱私」 標籤,會列出該 App 的資料處理摘要,與本政策一併適用;若有差異,以該 App 標籤所揭露者為準。

1. 我們的原則

本工作室的 App 以最小蒐集為原則。除了下方 「特定 App 的額外揭露」所列的 App 之外(且各 App 會在其 App Store 「App 隱私」標籤中揭露),本工作室的 App:

  • 不要求註冊帳號,不蒐集你的姓名、Email、電話等可識別個人身分的資料。
  • 不追蹤你跨 App 或跨網站的行為,也不將任何資料用於廣告投放。
  • 不販售你的個人資料給第三方。

2. 我們如何處理資料

若某個 App 需要存取裝置功能(例如相機、麥克風、相片、定位、通訊錄等),相關資料僅在你的裝置上、 為提供該功能而使用。除非該 App 明確說明,否則不會上傳到本工作室或任何第三方伺服器。

特定 App 的額外揭露

上述總則描述的是「不蒐集資料」的 App。下列 App 有額外的資料處理;對這些 App,本節揭露在總則之上 生效(更具體者以本節為準)。每一項都與該 App 的 App Store「App 隱私」標籤一致。

截至上方生效日,沒有任何 App 在總則之外蒐集資料。

3. 第三方服務

若個別 App 使用第三方服務(例如 Apple 提供的系統服務、當機回報或分析工具),會在該 App 內或其 App Store「App 隱私」標籤中揭露,並另適用該第三方自身的隱私政策。

4. 兒童隱私

本工作室的 App 不會在知情的情況下,蒐集 13 歲(或你所在地法律所定年齡)以下兒童的個人資料。

5. 資料保存與安全

凡在裝置上產生的資料,由你的裝置與作業系統管理。你可以隨時在裝置上刪除該 App, 以移除其在本機儲存的資料。

6. 你的權利

依你所在地的法律(例如 GDPR、CCPA),你可能享有查詢、更正、刪除個人資料等權利。由於本工作室 原則上不蒐集可識別個人身分的資料,通常我們不持有可供查詢的個人資料;如有疑問,請以下方方式 與我們聯絡。

7. 政策變更

本政策可能不定期更新;更新時會修改本頁內容與上方的「生效日期」。重大變更會在 App 內或商店頁 另行說明。
